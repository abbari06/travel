SRINAGAR TOUR PACKAGE WITH DOODPATHRI DETAILS

Day 1: Arrive Srinagar | Srinagar Local Sightseeing:

Welcome to the Srinagar, the Lake City. On arrival at Srinagar Airport, being your tour of Royal Kashmir Tour. You will be transferred to Hotel. Proceed on a city tour visiting the Shankracharya temple situated on the highest hill of Srinagar, the famous Mughal Gardens-Chashmashahi, Nishat & Shalimar. These gardens were Mughal Emperors concept of Paradise and today these are popular picnic spots. Also visit a handicraft shop where you can purchase local Hand Crafted items-Shawls, Jackets, Bed Covers, Paper Mache, Carpets & much more! In the evening leisurely stroll alongside the Dal Lake exploring the local markets. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 2: Srinagar-Gulmarg-Sonamarg

Next Day after breakfast Board Gondola cable car ride and visit Kongdori at phase 1 and Affarwat at phase 2. May do skiing/ sledging and snow biking at phase 1. Return to main town again May also explore Famous church, Temple, Ngeen valley and strawberry valley. Please note for local convenience one has to rely on Poneys and atv rides to explore other places in Gulmarg and one should always take advice from travel experts for Rates for the same to avoid exploitation. Later in evening drive towards sonamarg, The meadow of Gold via Sumbal byepass. A journey of 120 kms shall be covered in about 3 hour time.

Day 3: In-Sonamarg

Next day visit Thajwas Glacier on poneys and Zojila pass by hiring union taxi. Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Sonamarg-Srinagar

Return to srinagar next day. After reaching srinagar visit Nishat, Shalimar gardens. Post lunch visit Pari mahal and Tulip garden. Explore the local handicraft market in srinagar as well. Check in at hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5: Doodpathri Day Trip

This day is reserved for doodpathri day trip. Doodpathri is just 35 kms away from srinagar. After reaching doodpathri visit doodganga and enjoy the virgin beauty of doodpathri. return to srinagar later for overnight stay.

Day 6: Srinagar-Pahalgam

This day move towards pahalgam. On the way stop by at awantipora ruins. Visit Aru valley, Betaab valley and chandanwari in Pahalgam. Check in at Hotel for dinner and over night stay.

Day 7: In Pahalgam

This day is at leisure in Pahalgam. May visit Baisaran valley also called as mini Switzerland. One needs to hire poneys to visit Baisaran. One may also Famous Pahalgam club for all types of indoor games and activities.

Day 8: Pahalgam - Srinagar

Next day transfer to Houseboat in Nigeen lake. Get fresh and leave for one hour shikara ride in Dal lake. In evening enjoy the hustle and bustle at the Boulevard road Dal lake. Return to houseboat for Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 9: Return Home with Happy Memories:

After breakfast transfer to Srinagar Airport to board your flight with Everlasting Memories .
